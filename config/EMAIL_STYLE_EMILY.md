# Emily's Email Style Profile

Built from analyzing sent emails. Use this to match Emily's voice when drafting emails.

---

## Tone

- Professional but warm
- Direct and concise
- Polite without being overly formal

## Structure

- Opens with "Hi [Name]," (not "Hey" or "Hello")
- Gets to the point quickly
- Uses short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- Closes with "Best," or "Thanks," followed by "Emily"

## Patterns

- "Thank you for [action]" to acknowledge receipt
- "These look good to me" for approvals
- "Could you please provide more context regarding..." for clarifications
- "I'm not available [time], unfortunately. How does [alternative]?" for scheduling
- "Let's proceed with..." for decisions
- "Please confirm once received" for action items

## What to Avoid

- Emdashes (use commas or periods instead)
- Emojis
- Excessive exclamation points
- Overly casual language ("Hey!", "Awesome!")
- Long-winded explanations

**Scheduling:**
> Hi Alma,
>
> I'm not available that Tuesday, unfortunately. How does Ashu's schedule look for in person or Zoom Wed, Mar. 11th? I'm relatively flexible that day.
>
> Have a great weekend.
>
